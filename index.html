About M&M's:

M&M's are colorful button-shaped candies produced by Mars, Incorporated.
They were first introduced in 1941 and are now one of the most popular candies worldwide.
M&M's are known for their hard candy shell and chocolate filling.

Interesting Fact: M&M's are not only available in various flavors but also come in different colors, allowing consumers to choose their favorite color combinations.
Additionally, M&M's are often used in baking and as toppings for desserts due to their vibrant colors and delicious taste.
